# Quick Start

Getting started with the storm-research skill is simple. You just need to ask for deep research.

Try these trigger phrases:
* "deep research on..."
* "storm: investigate..."
* "comprehensive research brief on..."

Here is a concrete example you can paste right now:
"deep research on the impact of AI on healthcare diagnostics. Focus on FDA-approved imaging tools from the last three years."

What happens next?
1. The skill activates and might ask you a few clarifying questions.
2. It simulates multiple expert perspectives to gather, verify, and synthesize information.
3. You get a comprehensive briefing document.

Check your output folder when it finishes. You'll find a markdown brief and a fully formatted `index.html` file ready to share.
